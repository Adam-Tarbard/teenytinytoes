About us

I have been creating framed hand and feet impressions for over 14 years. Based just outside Petworth. I cover Sussex, Surrey & Hampshire and can create beautiful keepsakes of babies as young as a week old, toddlers, siblings, family, pet, first shoe and photo combinations.



Each frame I do is unique as is your little one. Impressions must be taken from the hand or foot to create a three dimensional cast that preserves the tiny lines on their hands, feet and toes – a gorgeous reminder of how little your baby and children once were.
