Foils


I can now also create personalised framed foil hand, foot or paw prints in a new cover-friendly and contactless way. An inkless wipe kit is sent to you so that you can take the prints in the comfort of your own home which you then send back to me for foiling and framing. 


There is a fantastic range of foil colours to choose from: rose gold, copper, silver, gold, black, teal, light blue, dark blue, light pink, dark pink, holographic, snake skin, leopard print, red, rainbow.


Please contact me on info@teenytinytoes.co.uk for prices on the foil prints you like.
