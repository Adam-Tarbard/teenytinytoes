Welcome to teeny tiny toes

Baby Hand & Foot Print Casting
About us

I have been creating framed hand and feet impressions for over 14 years. Based just outside Petworth. I cover Sussex, Surrey & Hampshire and can create beautiful keepsakes of babies as young as a week old, toddlers, siblings, family, pet, first shoe and photo combinations.



Each frame I do is as unique as your little one. Impressions must be taken from the hand or foot to create a three dimensional cast that preserves the tiny lines on their hands, feet and toes – a gorgeous reminder of how little your baby and children once were.


Please take a look in the photo gallery – this is just a small sample of the full range of work that can be produced. I’m more than happy to discuss your individual requirements with you to create that bespoke order giving you a precious memory that you can treasure forever.

I hope to see you soon.


Alex, teenytinytoes
